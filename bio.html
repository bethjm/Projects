Hi, I'm Beth...

I am a software engineer with a background in personal training and biomechanics. My core skills include JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Flexbox, and jQuery. I love hiking, cooking, and snuggling with my cats. I am most at peace in the mountains.

Why the transition from training to software?

In my practice as a trainer I am hired to create a product. Example: someone may want to cycle 400 miles uphill in 5 days (yes, this is true). During my evaluation I look for ‘bugs’ in the system, something that might be interrupting fluid movement and causing a problem.

I put together an exercise program to ‘debug’ the issues and pick the quickest and effective exercises. The exercises don't always work for a person's system, so I am adaptable in finding other solutions that acheive a similair result. After time, debugged, and patience- the client acheives the product they hired me for. (Can you see the similairities?)
