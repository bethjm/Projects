Hi, I'm Beth...

I am a software engineer with a background in personal training and biomechanics. My core skills include JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Flexbox, and jQuery. I love hiking, cooking, and snuggling with my cats. I am most at peace in the mountains.

Why the transition from training to software?

The application of biomechanics to personal training sparked my love for complex problem solving. In my practice I would look at the body as a whole system, not just "leg" "arm" "abs". The whole body works together to create pain free and effective movement. Through my evalualtion system, I find the "bugs" in a persons system that are impacting their movement. I then write a program to "debug" the root cause of the problem to help someone move pain free. (Can you see the similairities?)

The perfect marraige of skills...

I am excited that software engineering will lend to my love for complex systemic problem solving. I am hoping that in the future I can progress into a project managment role. I am very proud of my ability to push someone to the next level without pushing them over the edge. I believe that combining these two skills would make me a valuable asset to any companies managment team
